MUMBAIKAR

Mumbai(capital of Maharashtra) : A place where poverty is very high , which is around 20% but still known as the commercial capital of India . It has people from below poverty line to one of the richest ones in the world . Around 62% people in Mumbai are still living in slums which is a lot more the half of the population.

  • Area : 603.4 km²
  • Renowned places : Taj Hotel , CST , Sanjay Gandhi National park , etc

Along with these things Mumbai is very famous for its beaches. As Mumbai is at the Western coast of India it has a direct contact with the Arabian sea . Mumbai's ocean has a magical sunrise and sunset which has the ability to fill every broken heart.
