• Juhu is an upmarket neighbourhood of Mumbai.
  • . It is surrounded by the Arabian Sea to the west, Versova to the north, Santacruz and Vile Parle to the east and Khar to the south.
  • Juhu is among the most affluent areas of the city and home to many Bollywood celebrities.
  • The nearest railway stations are Santacruz, Andheri and Vile Parle on the Western Line and Harbour Line of the Mumbai Suburban Railway.
  • The nearest metro station is D. N. Nagar. There are two minor B.E.S.T bus depots in Juhu.
